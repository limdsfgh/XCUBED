ABOUT US

We are a creative team specializing in UI/UX, photography, and graphic design, including logos and posters. Our work combines creativity, functionality, and visual impact, reflecting our unique style and attention to detail. We strive to push creative boundaries and bring ideas to life in every project.

Our Team

Lim Xin Le

I’m Lim Xin Le, the CEO and Photographer at XCUBED. I lead the company while capturing moments and telling stories through my photography. My work focuses on composition, lighting, and color to create images that are both visually striking and meaningful. I enjoy finding unique perspectives and making every shot convey a story that connects with viewers.

At XCUBED, I work closely with my team to ensure our photography aligns with the overall design and branding of our projects. I pay attention to every detail—from framing to post-processing—to create visuals that not only look stunning but also enhance the experience for our audience. My goal is to produce photography that inspires, communicates effectively, and leaves a lasting impression, while guiding XCUBED’s vision and growth.

Chin Xue Jing

I’m Chin Xue Jing, a Graphic Designer at XCUBED. I bring ideas to life through visual design, using color, typography, and imagery to create designs that are clear, attractive, and easy to understand. I focus on clean and modern styles, making sure every element is consistent and well-organized.

At XCUBED, I work closely with my teammates to make sure each project feels balanced and visually cohesive. I pay attention to details in layout, color, and overall composition to ensure the designs not only look great but also enhance the user experience. My goal is to craft visuals that communicate effectively, engage users, and leave a lasting impression.

Tan Xin Yee

I’m Tan Xin Yee, a UI/UX Designer at XCUBED. I design interfaces that make complex ideas easy to understand and use. I enjoy creating clean, modern layouts and pay close attention to details, ensuring every design is consistent, organized, and visually appealing.

At XCUBED, I work closely with our team to bring balance and harmony to each project. I focus on both aesthetics and user experience, aiming to make every interaction smooth, intuitive, and enjoyable. My goal is to create products that are practical, visually engaging, and provide a seamless experience for all users.
